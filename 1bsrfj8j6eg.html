6. Interpretation and Policy Relevance

Cash transfers to poor families ameliorated early life conditions enough to improve both medium- and long-term outcomes of boys growing up in poverty. First assessment of unconditional cash transfers. A new approach for identification and long-term data accessibility. Short- and medium-term effects are in line with contemporary research about the impact of cash transfers on education and health outcomes.
