5.3 Health

To obtain health results, a subsample of the MP records has to be matched with their WWII enlistment records for the cohort that enlisted the Army during 1938-1946. However, the match rate to the records is rather low (17.2% of males overall) since the records do not contain exact date of birth. Furthermore, it has to be noted that participants in the enlistment are a selected subsample of the population because of induction rule
