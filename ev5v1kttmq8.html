Panel B of Table 4 presents the results from the Logit model. Under the assumption that those who without a match died until the age of 60 ((1)-(4)) and dropping all of them in specification (5), the role of attrition can be assessed. The probability of surviving past the age of 70 increases significantly for all specifications and is estimated to have a percent effect of 11% - 19%.
