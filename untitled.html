1. Introduction 

More than 20% of children in the United States were living in poverty as recently as 2010.  Evidence of Parental Income influencing children’s income, educational attainment, and health. Research Question: What is the long-run impact of cash transfers to poor families on children’s longevity, educational attainment, income, and health?
Identifies a plausible counterfactual. Data on long-term outcomes. No eligibility for other programs. Isolating the effect of cash transfers.
